Dream

Being able to use my programming skills to improve my society Or make a shit ton of money by programming and then ... use it for the betterment of the society. I have this dream or a goal. Operation B2C wherein I would like to create a business model where I can turn the beggars in our country into working citizens. B Giving poor people fridges, televisions, grinders, gold etc does not alleviate them from poverty (like how we do it in our state tamil nadu, india ) , but teaching them how to earn, sure does put them in the right way to go beyond surviving into living. My model is similar to this. Earn shit ton of money, buy 10 to 100 acres of land. Employ only beggars. The food from these farms will be used for their food. And they will be given clothing. So I will have a small team of tailors stitching clothes for them. I will make a makeshift shelter made out of coconut leaves patched up. I think costs upto $200 a house to make in india and in other developing countries. And in a few months, we get them jobs in other industries where they need workers desperately. And then I would take in a next batch of beggars and then do the same whole process stated above. When I am able to do this in each and every state in my country, then I would be able to get rid of poverty within 10 years. Compounding effect. And if successful , create several models in every state. This way, we teach beggars about how to work and earn their food. And they will be sent to other places where workers are needed. So in effect we are bringing people out of the poverty line, reducing unemployment and at the same time, giving industries manpower when they are in need of it desperately. So in effect we are converting "Beggars" into "Citizens". Hence the name of the operation B2C. I am sure this can be applied in other countries too. If you think this is a worthy goal or you would like to do something similar too. Then I request you to please upvote this answer. And also to provide valuable feedback cause this is just a framework for getting rid of the poverty problem. What it needs now is a lot of suggestions and feedback. And if any body else does come across a lot of money and are not sure what to do with it. You are welcome to implement my Operation. I would just be really really glad. I just wish every filthy rich person in our country thought of this as one of their goals. Then we wouldn't have any poverty at all. All the best.
